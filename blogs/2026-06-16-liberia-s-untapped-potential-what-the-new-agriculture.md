Liberia's new Agriculture Census 2022/23 and the 2024 Annual Agriculture Survey provide the most comprehensive picture of the country's agricultural sector in more than fifty years. The data reveals underutilized land resources, limited adoption of modern technologies, and barriers to transformation in a predominantly subsistence-based farming system.
